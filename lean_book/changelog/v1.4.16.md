Chapter 1 §1 (`Nat` as an initial algebra / natural numbers object)

informal `α`/`β` slash notation inside otherwise formal mathematical
prose. No factual or citation content changed.

Also ran a full-book audit: checked all 112 markdown files for broken
relative links (0 found), dangling citation keys with no bibliography
